City of Iowa City MLK Day Holiday Schedule
Issued by
Communications
on Jan 11, 2019.

Administrative offices at City Hall will be closed on Monday, Jan. 21 2019, in observance of Martin Luther King, Jr. Day. Note: Curbside garbage, organics and recycling will be collected on Martin Luther King Jr. Day. Customers on the Monday routes should have items to the curb per their normal schedule.

View the full schedule: https://www.icgov.org/news/closures-announced-martin-luther-king-jr-day
